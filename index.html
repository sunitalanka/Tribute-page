Mahatma Gandhi

Father of the Nation

Here's a time line of Mahatma Gandhi's life:


  • 1869 - Birth at Porbandar India.
  • 1882 - Marriage with Kasturbai, aged 13.
  • 1888 - Reached Southampton, England
  • 1888 - Enrolled at the Inner Temple, Inn of Court
  • 1894 - Founded the 'Natal Indian Congress'.
  • 1903 - First issue of 'Indian Opinion' published.
  • 1906 - Advent of Satyagraha at Empire Theatre, Johannesburg, where people took a pledge with God as their witness, to oppose the Black Act.
  • 1908 - The term 'Satyagraha' adopted based on Maganlal Gandhi's fomulation 'Sadagraha'.
  • 1909 - 'Hind Swarajya' published in 'Indian Opinion'.
  • 1913 - MKG released from jail unconditionally with others, before the completion of the period of imprisonement.
  • 1914 - Indians' Relief Bill (1914), passed.
  • 1915 - Established 'Satyagraha Ashram', Kochrab.
  • 1915 - Elected as Vice President of Gujarat Sabha.
  • 1916 - Met Jawaharlal Nehru for the first time at Lucknow Congress.
  • 1917 - Sabarmati Ashram established.
  • 1918 - Appointed to arbitar the dispute between Mill-owners and Mill-hands of Ahmedabad.
  • 1918 - Indefinite fast to keep the resolve of striking Mill-hands commenced.
  • 1919 - Satyagraha pledge against 'Rowlett Act'.
  • 1925 - 'All India Spinners' Association' established.
  • 1930 - Dandi March commences from Satyagraha Ashram.
  • 1933 - Commneced publication of 'Harijan'. 'Harijan Sevak' (Hindi), commenced publication on 23rd February and 'Harijanbandhu' (Gujarati) commenced publication on 12th March.
  • 1940 - Congress Working Committee meeting at Sevagram; Individual Satyagraha proposed.
  • 1941 - The Congress Working Committee at Bardoli accepted Gandhiji's request to free him from the responsibility of guiding the Congress.
  • 1942 - Congress Working Committee adopted the resolution for immediate withdrawl of the British from India.
  • 1942 - 'Quit India' resolution adopted; gave the call 'Do or Die'.
  • 1942 - Gandhiji and Congress Working Committee members arrested; Gandhiji imprisoned at the Aga Khan Palace.
  • 1945 - Congress Working Committee members released from Ahmedanagar Fort Prison. Ban on Congress lifted.
  • 1946 - Three member British delegation reaches Delhi.
  • 1946 -The Congress Working Committee adopted resolution to accept the formation of the Constituent Assembly.
  • 1946 - A 12 member Interim Government headed by Pandit Nehru formed.
  • 1946 - Muslim League representatives joined the Interim Government.
  • 1947 - Addressed the Asian Relations Conference.
  • 1947 - The Congress Working Committee adopted resolution to accept the Partition.
  • 1948 - A bomb thrown during the Prayer Meeting.
  • 1948 - Took three bullets on his chest. Godse the assassin.
  • 1948 - Cremated on the banks of the Yamuna by Ramdas (son).
